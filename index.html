Crash on a volcano

One volcano in the center, 9 rovers which rush over it. Meanwhile, other rovers risk collision : What will happen ? (worst synopsis ever :o).

Press the "Prepare exploration" to initialize the exploration trip and "Launch exploration" to launch it. Get detailed moves in console.

Dance of the rovers

Six rovers dance around 4 volcanos. They all make a complete tour then move away the center.

Press the "Prepare exploration" to initialize the exploration trip and "Launch exploration" to launch it. Get detailed moves in console.

Your own exploration !

You can create your own exploration : choose grid size, obstacle and rover position.

Always press "Clear exploration" if you want to create a new exploration, otherwise, previous parameters will still be in memory.

Press "Launch exploration" to launch your exploration. Get detailed moves in console.
